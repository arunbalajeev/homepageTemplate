Motion Characterization of a Dynamic Scene

Arun Balajee V, Srikanth M, Shiva pratheek Ch, Dr. Shanmuganathan Raman

The proposed work is focused on classifying the dynamic objects as having either repetitive or non-repetitive motion. In this work, we propose a novel approach to achieve this challenging task by processing the optical flow fields corresponding to the video frames of a dynamic natural scene.

Multiple Object Localization using Saliency Detection

Srikanth M, Arun Balajee V, Shiva pratheek Ch, Dr. Shanmuganathan Raman

This paper describes a novel approach for localization of multiple objects from a given image of a natural scene. Multiple objects are localized by the application of saliency detection-superpixels on the saliency map and the other is application of active contour model on the saliency map.
